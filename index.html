Who We Are

LEX CORP IS A DISTINGUISHED BOUTIQUE INDIAN LAW FIRM Founded in 2010, Lex Corp is a young and dynamic law firm built on the tenets of exemplary legal practice, a passion for the work we do, and an unwavering commitment to cater to and align the interests of our clients.

With our legal acumen, expertise and diverse experience, we are able to serve the unique needs of each client. We diligently work with our clients to help them navigate through the maze of complex legal issues successfully.

About Lex Corp

Our mission is to prove ourselves to be trusted legal advisors to our clients and provide them with outstanding and innovative legal solutions in our chosen practice areas, all the while placing a heavy emphasis on our ethical and professional responsibility, both towards our clients as well as our own team members.

Meet The Team

Our team comprises of talented and focused lawyers with diverse expertise, who have been consistently delivering best in class litigation and consultancy service At Lex Corp, we strive to help our clients navigate through the complex and multifaceted Indian legal system and negotiate their way around the business ecosystem prevalent in India successfully.

Services

We provide end to end legal solutions and advisory across industries and diverse practice areas such as Insolvency, Banking & Finance including Bank Recoveries under RDDBFI Act & SARFAESI Act; Real Estate; Taxation, Dispute Resolution & Arbitration; General Corporate Commercial; Family Law; Intellectual Property Rights; And other Civil & Criminal matters.
